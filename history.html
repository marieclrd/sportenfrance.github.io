Our history

Founded in 2010, SPORT EN FRANCE was created by 2 best friends, both sports fans with overflowing imaginations! Julien CAPIN and Johan RICHARD both hail from a small province in the south of France, called Ollioules!

Close to both the sea and the mountains, our two southerners have had the chance to try out hundreds of different sports! So they set up SPORT EN FRANCE with the aim of bringing together all the equipment needed to practice any sport! Try your hand at cycling, swimming, padel, badminton, running or golf!

Every day, we try to support you in your sporting endeavors! Trust us, we trust you!
